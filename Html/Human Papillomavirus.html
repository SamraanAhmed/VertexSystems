HPV (Human Papillomavirus)

Overview

Introduction:

HPV causes warts and cancers (cervical 90%); most clear naturally. Vaccine safe, recommended 9-45; 2025 data shows mild side effects, no reproductive issues. Over 200 types.

Cause:

Over 200 HPV types; high-risk (16/18) cause cancer, low-risk warts. Non-enveloped DNA virus.

Types of HPV

Low-risk (warts), high-risk (cancer); vaccine covers 9 types. 2025 safety: rare serious events (7.8% Gardasil-9).

  • Types 6, 11 cause 90% genital warts.
  • Benign, no cancer risk.
  • Common, self-resolve.
  • Vaccine prevents.
  • Types 16, 18 cause 70% cervical cancers.
  • Also anal, oral, penile.
  • Persistent infection key risk.
  • Screening detects precancer.

Structure / Nature of HPV Virus

Non-enveloped icosahedral DNA virus in Papillomaviridae; replicates in epithelial cells.

  • DNA Genome: Circular double-stranded DNA (~8 kb).
  • Capsid: 72 pentamers, L1/L2 proteins.
  • Shape: Non-enveloped, 50-60 nm.

Diseases / Complications

Warts benign; high-risk leads to cancer if persistent. Vaccine prevents 90% cervical. 2025: no proven reproductive risks.

Mild / Benign
  • Genital warts
  • Skin warts
  • Recurrent respiratory (rare)
Severe Complications
  • Cervical cancer
  • Anal/oral cancers
  • Oropharyngeal cancer
High-Risk Groups

More likely to develop complications:

  • Women (cervical screening)
  • MSM (anal cancer)
  • Smokers
  • Immunosuppressed
  • Multiple partners

Prevention

1
HPV vaccine

Gardasil-9 for 9-45; 2-3 doses.

2
Condoms

Reduce but not eliminate risk.

3
Regular screening

Pap/HPV tests for cervical cancer.

4
Fewer partners

Lowers exposure risk.

5
Avoid smoking

Increases cancer risk with HPV.

6
Delay sexual debut

Reduces lifetime exposure.

Antiviral Medications

No antivirals; virus clears naturally. Treat symptoms: cryotherapy, imiquimod for warts.

Severe Cases

Surgery/radiation for cancers; LEEP/cone for precancer. 2025: vaccine safe, rare AEs.

  • Wart removal options
  • Cancer treatment multimodal
No virus cure; prevent with vaccine/screening.
