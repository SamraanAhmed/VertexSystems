Beginner's Guide

Simple notes to help you understand the list and why these items matter.

2
How to read deaths: These are rough historical estimates; exact counts can be uncertain.
3
Prevention basics: Simple measures like handwashing, vaccination (when available), and staying home when sick help reduce spread.
